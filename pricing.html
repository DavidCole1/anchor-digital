FAQ

Common questions

Do I have to pay before I see anything?
No. You get a free one-page sample in your own name and branding first. You only pay if you want the full site built.
I already have a website — is this still useful?
Yes, if it's outdated or not bringing in calls. I can rebuild it or start you on a Care Plan to fix and maintain what's there.
If AI builds it, how is it actually "custom"?
AI drafts the structure, copy, and code fast — but every site is built around your actual business, photos, and branding, then reviewed and adjusted by hand before launch. Nothing goes live unedited.
What happens after the site launches?
The Care Plan starts month two: monthly checks for broken links and speed, up to 3 small content updates (hours, prices, photos, text), and a plain-English report so you always know what changed.
